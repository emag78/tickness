Privacy Policy

Tickness is committed to respecting your privacy and protecting your personal data.

1. Data collected

The app does not collect or store any personal data on external servers. All data is saved locally on your device.

2. Use of microphone

The microphone permission is used solely to allow users to dictate and transcribe notes within the app. No audio is recorded or transmitted to external servers.

3. Calendar integration

If you choose to export a task to your calendar, the app will ask for permission to add events to your device's calendar. This data remains on your device and is not shared.

4. No tracking

The app does not include advertising, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

5. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact me at: enrico.magrella@gmail.com
